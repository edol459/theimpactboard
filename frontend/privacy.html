Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 4, 2025

ydkball is an NBA game review and discussion platform. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights around it. We don't sell your data and we don't run ads.

Information We Collect

When you create an account and use ydkball, we collect:

  • Account information — your display name and email address used to sign in
  • Profile content — your profile photo, favorite team, and favorite games if you choose to set them
  • Reviews and ratings — game reviews, star ratings, player tags, and attended status that you submit
  • Social activity — friend connections you make, likes you give, and replies you post
  • Usage data — basic server logs (IP address, timestamps, pages visited) for security and debugging purposes

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Operate your account and display your profile
  • Show your reviews, ratings, and activity to other users
  • Power the Friends feed and social features
  • Respond to support requests
  • Monitor for abuse and keep the platform secure

We do not use your data for advertising, sell it to third parties, or share it with anyone except as described in this policy.

What's Public

By default, the following information is visible to other logged-in users of ydkball:

  • Your display name and profile photo
  • Your reviews, ratings, and replies
  • Your favorite team and favorite games
  • Your friend count

Your email address is never shown publicly.

Data Storage

Your data is stored on servers in the United States. We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. Server logs are retained for a limited period for security purposes and then deleted.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Update your display name and profile photo at any time from your profile page
  • Delete individual reviews from the game's review page
  • Request deletion of your entire account and all associated data by emailing us

To request account deletion, contact us at ydkbball@gmail.com. We will process the request within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

ydkball is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we'll update the date at the top. Continued use of ydkball after changes means you accept the updated policy.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at ydkbball@gmail.com.
